About

Hey, nice to meet you. I graduated from the Department of Journalism at Konkuk University and received my Master's degree in Journalism from the Graduate School of Konkuk University, where I also completed my PhD.

I have worked as a senior researcher at the Korea Internet & Security Agency and as an expert member of the LocalNewspaper Development Committee. Currently, I am a Research Fellow at the Newspaper Development Research Institute of the Korean Newspaper Association and a Senior Researcher at DCRD(the Center for Digital Communication Research) at Konkuk University.

My work mainly involves researching regional dynamics, spatial relations, journalism and media policy for the development of domestic journalism.

Thank you for visiting my website and for your interest in my work and research. Please feel free to explore the site to learn more about my studies and contributions in the fields of regional dynamics, spatial relations, media and journalism. If you have any questions or would like to engage in a meaningful discussion, please do not hesitate to contact me. Your engagement and feedback is invaluable.
